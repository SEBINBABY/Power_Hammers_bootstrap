Commercial Construction


POWER HAMMER BUILDERS is one of the largest and most respected civil construction
companies in California, with a global presence and a track record of delivering landmark
projects in various segments. POWER HAMMER’s commercial construction portfolio
includes airports, bridges, defence installations, factories, hospitals, housing, metros,
office spaces, ports, railways, smart world solutions, transportation infrastructure,
underground structures, water infrastructure and more. POWER HAMMER BUILDERS
offers end-to-end solutions for commercial construction projects, from design and
engineering to procurement and construction. POWER HAMMER leverages its unique
capabilities, innovation centres and cutting-edge techniques to execute complex and
challenging projects with high quality and safety standards.

Residential Construction


POWER HAMMER BUILDERS is one of the leading real estate developers and
builders in California, with a portfolio of residential projects in Los Angeles, San Jose,
an Francisco and San Diego. POWER HAMMER BUILDERS’s residential construction
covers two categories - Elite Housing and Affordable & Mass Housing. POWER
HAMMER BUILDERS’s expertise in residential buildings includes design and
construction of large townships and high-rise apartments, using quality materials and equipping the edifices with ultra-modern home automation systems. POWER HAMMER BUILDERS offers end-to-end solutions for residential construction projects,from concept
to completion, with a focus on customer satisfaction and environmental sustainability.

Landscape Construction


POWER HAMMER BUILDERS is one of the leading landscape construction companies
in India, with a vision to create green and sustainable spaces for living, working and
recreation. POWER HAMMER BUILDERS’s landscape construction services include
master planning, design, execution and maintenance of various types of landscapes,
such as gardens, parks, campuses, resorts, golf courses, waterfronts, urban forests and
more. POWER HAMMER has a team of experienced and qualified landscape architects,
engineers, horticulturists and environmentalists who work together to deliver innovative
and aesthetic solutions for diverse landscape projects. POWER HAMMER BUILDERS
uses advanced technologies and best practices to ensure quality, safety and
environmental compliance in its landscape construction activities.

Pre-Construction


POWER HAMMER BUILDERS is one of the pioneers in adopting precast technology
for its construction projects, which can enhance the speed, quality, safety and
sustainability of the structures. Precast technology involves casting concrete elements
in a factory and transporting them to the site for assembly. POWER HAMMER BUILDERS
has set up dedicated precast yards at various locations in India and abroad, equipped
with state-of-the-art machinery and skilled manpower. POWER HAMMER BUILDERS’s
precast technology can be used across multiple applications to build large span storage
and factory structures, long span marine and bridge structures, large span buildings like theatres, auditoria, stadia, hangars including hi-rise residential structures and office
complexes.

Site Management


POWER HAMMER BUILDERS’s site-management involves planning, organizing, directing,
controlling and coordinating the activities of construction projects, from inception to
completion, to ensure that they are completed on time, within budget and according to
specifications. POWER HAMMER BUILDERS’s site-management team consists of
qualified and experienced professionals who are responsible for overseeing the
execution of work, ensuring quality and safety standards, managing resources and
materials, resolving issues and conflicts, communicating with stakeholders and
reporting progress.
