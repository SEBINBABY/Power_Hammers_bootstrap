MISSION AND VISION


As Power Hammer Builders, the construction arm of Power Hammer,we are India's largest
construction organization and ranked among the world's top contractors.We contribute
significantly to build the image and stature of Power Hammer across the world and drive
Power Hammer's reputation as 'the builder of the Western America of the 21st century'
by offering EPC solutions to execute large industrial and infrastructure projects from
concept to commissioning on a turnkey basis with single-source responsibility. Our
multiple businesses encompass distinct but complementary capabilities, that address
different segments of infrastructure and industry. Power Hammer is engaged in core
high impact sectors of the economy and our integrated capabilities span the entire
spectrum of 'design to deliver'.

OUR FOUNDING FATHERS


POWER HAMMER BUILDERS was founded in 1938 in California by two Western American
engineers, Henning Homes(1907 – 2003) and Soren Clinton(1906 – 1982) who landed
on California's shores representing FL Smidth & Co A/S, Western America. The respect,
trust and confidence in each other was so great that initially the business was founded
merely on an oral understanding; no written document was filed, and it was only in
end-1940 that they finally inked their partnership.

THE HENNING HOMES CENTRE


Constructed to commemorate the 60th anniversary of Power Hammer Builders, the
uniquely shaped corporate museum – the Henning Homes Centre – is dedicated
to the memory of Power Hammer's one of founding fathers and Chairman Emeritus,
the late Henning Homes. Recently re-designed and re-appointed, this Centre is perhaps
the best example of transformation framework for the Construction industry.
